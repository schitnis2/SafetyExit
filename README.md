# SafeExit — Safety Companion PWA

A discreet progressive web app for domestic violence safety planning.

## Features
- Disguise mode (looks like a homework app)
- Rotating daily affirmations
- Shelter finder with real GPS location support
- Safety checklist (saved across sessions)
- Private incident log (saved to device, never uploaded)
- One-tap hotline calling

## How to deploy (GitHub Pages — free, no coding needed)

### Step 1 — Create a GitHub account
1. Go to github.com
2. Click "Sign up" and create a free account

### Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the green "New" button on your dashboard
2. Name it: safeexit (all lowercase)
3. Set it to Public
4. Click "Create repository"

### Step 3 — Upload the files
1. On your new repository page, click "uploading an existing file"
2. Drag ALL four files into the upload box:
   - index.html
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
   - README.md
3. Scroll down and click "Commit changes"

### Step 4 — Enable GitHub Pages
1. Click the "Settings" tab at the top of your repo
2. In the left sidebar, click "Pages"
3. Under "Source", select "Deploy from a branch"
4. Under "Branch", choose "main" and folder "/ (root)"
5. Click "Save"

### Step 5 — Get your live link
1. Wait 1–2 minutes
2. Refresh the Pages settings page
3. Your app will be live at: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/safeexit

### Step 6 — Add to phone home screen (makes it feel like a real app)
On iPhone:
1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

On Android:
1. Open in Chrome
2. Tap the three dots menu
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
